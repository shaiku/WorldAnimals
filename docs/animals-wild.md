# Wild Animals

This page covers all non-tameable wild animals in the World Animals addon. These creatures range from peaceful herbivores to dangerous predators, adding diversity and challenge to your world.

## Passive Animals

These animals are peaceful and won't attack under normal circumstances.

### Deer

![Deers Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-deers-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Forests, grasslands, meadows
- **Health:** 12 HP
- **Behavior:** Flee from players, travel in herds
- **Drops:** Venison, hide
- **Special:** Fast runners when startled
- **Appearance:** Various brown colorations

### Zebra

![Zebras Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-zebras-screenshot.webp)

![Zebra Decorations Recipes](images/world-animals-addon-zebra-decorations-recipes.webp)

![Zebra Decorations Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-zebra-decorations-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Savanna, grasslands
- **Health:** 25 HP
- **Behavior:** Travel in herds, flee from threats
- **Drops:** Zebra skin, raw meat
- **Special:** Distinctive stripes, can be used for decorative items (carpets, sofas)
- **Notable:** Materials used for unique zebra-themed furniture

### Flamingo

![Flamingos Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-flamingos-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Tropical swamps, beaches, warm waters
- **Health:** 8 HP
- **Behavior:** Wading birds, peaceful
- **Drops:** Feathers, colorful feathers
- **Special:** Pink coloring, stands on one leg
- **Appearance:** Iconic pink plumage

### Duck

![Ducks Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-ducks-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Lakes, rivers, water biomes
- **Health:** 6 HP
- **Behavior:** Swim peacefully, waddle on land
- **Drops:** Raw duck meat, feathers, eggs
- **Food Item:** Can be cooked into food
- **Special:** Aquatic birds, lays eggs
- **Appearance:** Waterfowl with typical duck coloring

### Snail

![Snails Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-snails-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Swamps, wet areas
- **Health:** 2 HP (very fragile)
- **Behavior:** Slowly move around, hide in shells
- **Drops:** Slime, shell fragments
- **Special:** Extremely slow movement
- **Appearance:** Gastropod with visible shell

### Squirrel

![Squirrels Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-squirrels-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Forests, temperate woodlands
- **Health:** 4 HP
- **Behavior:** Climb trees, store nuts, agile

- **Appearance:** Small fluffy rodents with bushy tails

### Buffalo

![Buffaloes Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-buffaloes-screenshot.webp)

- **Health:** 35 HP

## Neutral Animals

These animals are generally peaceful but may attack if provoked or threatened.

### Bear / Black Bear

![Black Bears Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-black-bears-in-the-forest-screenshot.webp)

![Brown Bears Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-brown-bears-in-the-forest-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Forests, taiga, mountain forests
- **Health:** 40 HP
- **Damage:** 10
- **Behavior:** Forage peacefully, aggressive if approached with cubs or threatened
- **Drops:** Raw meat, hide, claws
- **Special:** Mother bears protect cubs
- **Variants:** Black bears and brown bears
- **Caution:** Keep distance from cubs to avoid aggression

### Hippopotamus

![Hippopotamus Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-hippopotamus-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Rivers, swamps, water areas in hot biomes
- **Health:** 50 HP
- **Damage:** 12
- **Behavior:** Spend most time in water, territorial
- **Drops:** Raw meat, hide, tusks
- **Special:** Powerful jaws, dangerous in water
- **Caution:** Avoid getting too close in their water territory

### Kangaroo

![Kangaroos Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-kangaroos-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Grasslands, savannas, plains
- **Health:** 20 HP
- **Damage:** 6
- **Behavior:** Bounce around, flee if attacked
- **Drops:** Raw meat, hide
- **Special:** Powerful jumping ability
- **Appearance:** Iconic hopping marsupials

### Wild Boar

![Wild Boar Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-wild-boar-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Forests, grasslands, dark oak forests
- **Health:** 25 HP
- **Damage:** 8
- **Behavior:** Forage on ground, aggressive if threatened
- **Drops:** Raw meat, hide, tusks
- **Special:** Tusks can be dangerous
- **Caution:** Don't provoke - they will charge

### Ant

![Ants Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-ants-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** All biomes on ground
- **Health:** 1 HP (very fragile)
- **Damage:** Negligible
- **Behavior:** Swarm behavior, work in colonies
- **Drops:** Chitin, small items
- **Special:** Extremely small creatures
- **Appearance:** Tiny insects, easy to miss

## Hostile Animals

These animals are inherently aggressive and will attack on sight.

### Crocodile

![Crocodile Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-crocodile-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Swamps, rivers, tropical waters
- **Health:** 30 HP
- **Damage:** 8
- **Behavior:** Aggressive ambush predators
- **Drops:** Crocodile hide, teeth, raw meat
- **Special:** Can snap at players from water
- **Variant:** 10% chance to spawn as white variant
- **Caution:** Avoid water with crocodiles

### Hyena

![Hyenas Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-hyenas-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Savannas, grasslands, deserts
- **Health:** 18 HP
- **Damage:** 6
- **Behavior:** Hunt in packs, aggressive
- **Drops:** Raw meat, hide
- **Special:** Pack hunting behavior
- **Caution:** Dangerous in groups

### Shark (Blue Shark)

![Blue Shark Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-blue-shark-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Ocean, deep water
- **Health:** 35 HP
- **Damage:** 10
- **Behavior:** Aggressive in water, hunt players
- **Drops:** Shark teeth, raw fish, meat
- **Special:** Can be crafted into weapons
- **Caution:** Extremely dangerous in water

### Shark (White Shark)

![White Shark Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-white-shark-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Ocean, deep water (rare)
- **Health:** 45 HP
- **Damage:** 12

### Shark (Tiger Shark)

![Tiger Shark Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-tiger-shark-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Tropical ocean, warm waters
- **Health:** 38 HP

- **Behavior:** Aggressive, pack hunters

## Aquatic Animals

These animals live primarily in water environments.

### Whale

![Whale Screenshot](images/world-animals-addon-whale-screenshot.webp)

- **Biomes:** Ocean, deep water
- **Health:** 100 HP
- **Damage:** Passive (but dangerous when provoked)
- **Behavior:** Peaceful swimmers, breach occasionally
- **Drops:** Whale meat, blubber, bone
- **Special:** Largest animal in addon
- **Caution:** Killing a whale gives Bad Omen and Weakness for 1 hour!

## Animal Drop Coordination

Some wild animals have special drop mechanics:

- **Stork & Pigeon:** Carry bags that drop upon death
- **Drowned Mobs:** Can spawn naturally with shark weapons
- **Whale:** Applies Bad Omen and Weakness status if killed
- **Lantern Fish:** Grants temporary Night Vision when eaten
