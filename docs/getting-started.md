# Getting Started with World Animals

This guide covers the essential mechanics you need to know to start playing with World Animals: taming, breeding, and eggs.

## Taming Animals

### The Golden Bone

The **Golden Bone** is the universal taming item for most tameable animals in the addon. It works like a magical bond that establishes trust between you and the animal.

#### Golden Bone Recipe

![Golden Bone Recipe](images/world-animals-addon-golden-bone-recipe.webp)

**Crafted with:**
- 8 Gold Ingots arranged in a square
- 1 Bone in the center

**Result:** 1 Golden Bone

### How to Tame

1. **Obtain a Golden Bone** using the recipe above
2. **Find a tameable animal** in the world (see [Tameable Animals](animals-tameable.md) for locations)
3. **Hold the Golden Bone** in your hand
4. **Right-click/interact** with the animal
5. **Watch for heart particles** - these indicate successful taming
6. **Interact again** to mount, open inventory, or access equipment options

### Taming Indicators

- **Hearts appearing** = Successful taming in progress
- **No response** = Animal not tameable with Golden Bone (some require specific foods)
- **Red particles** = Animal is hostile or already wild

## Breeding

### Feeding for Breeding

Once you have at least two tamed animals of the same species, you can breed them:

1. **Tame two animals** of the same species
2. **Feed both animals** their preferred food (varies by species - see [Tameable Animals](animals-tameable.md))
3. **Watch for hearts** - animals enter breeding mode when well-fed
4. **A baby will spawn** nearby once breeding is complete
5. **Baby grows** to adulthood over time, can be accelerated with Gold Bone Meal

### Growth Acceleration

Use **Gold Bone Meal** to speed up animal growth significantly.

#### Gold Bone Meal Recipe

![Gold Bone Meal Recipe](images/world-animals-addon-gold-bone-meal-recipe.webp)

**Crafted with:**
- 9 Gold Nuggets arranged in a square

**Result:** 1 Gold Bone Meal

### Using Gold Bone Meal

1. **Craft or obtain Gold Bone Meal**
2. **Hold it in your hand**
3. **Right-click/interact on a baby animal** or placed egg
4. **The animal grows instantly** or significantly faster
5. **Multiple uses** on eggs accelerate hatching

## Eggs

Some animals lay eggs or can be bred to produce eggs. Eggs can hatch naturally or be accelerated with Gold Bone Meal.

### Egg Mechanics

- **Placement:** Eggs must be placed on solid ground blocks
- **Natural Hatching:** Eggs hatch over time automatically
- **Acceleration:** Use Gold Bone Meal to speed up hatching significantly
- **Growth:** Baby animals hatch from eggs and grow to adulthood

### Hatching Process

1. **Find or obtain an animal egg** (some animals lay them, others are crafted)
2. **Place the egg** on the ground in a suitable area
3. **Wait for hatching** - this takes time depending on the animal
4. **Use Gold Bone Meal** to speed up the process (right-click the egg)
5. **Baby spawns** when hatching is complete
6. **Growth continues** - the baby grows into an adult animal

## Sugar Cubes (Elephant Taming)

Some animals like Elephants have alternative taming methods. **Sugar Cubes** are a special food item for specific animals.

#### Sugar Cube Recipe

![Sugar Cube Recipe](images/world-animals-addon-beta-sugar-cube-recipe.webp)

**Crafted with:**
- 9 Sugar arranged in a square

**Result:** 1 Sugar Cube

**Usage:** Use Sugar Cubes when Golden Bones alone aren't sufficient for taming certain animals, or to speed up breeding in specific species.

## Food Types

Different animals prefer different foods for breeding:

- **Meat Eaters:** Raw or cooked meat, fish, raw ostrich legs, etc.
- **Herbivores:** Fruits, vegetables, seeds, hay, wheat
- **Omnivores:** Both meat and plant-based foods
- **Specialized Diets:** Some animals eat specific items like bananas, honey, or melons

Refer to [Tameable Animals](animals-tameable.md) for each animal's specific food preferences.

## Common Tips

- **Taming takes patience** - stay calm and keep trying with the Golden Bone
- **Breeding requires two tamed animals** - you'll need to tame at least two before breeding is possible
- **Baby animals grow over time** - Gold Bone Meal significantly speeds this up
- **Eggs are fragile** - protect them from harm while they hatch
- **Different biomes have different animals** - explore to find all species
